FAQ

Is this medical advice?

No. I don’t give medical advice. If you need detox or medical help, I’ll point you to professionals who do.

Do you promise results?

No. Sobriety can never be guaranteed. I guarantee honesty, no judgment, and support to find what fits your life.

What if I relapse?

We aim to prevent it. If it happens, you’re welcome to come back. We learn and try again — until you’re sober.

Do you only work with alcohol?

Alcohol is my main focus. I can support other addictions if needed.

Is it confidential?

Yes. 100% private. Everything we discuss stays between us.

How do sessions work?

One‑on‑one video calls. Between sessions, we may agree on short chat check‑ins for difficult times.
